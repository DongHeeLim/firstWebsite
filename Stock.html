Stock

Adjustable Stock

3d printer material is PETG. It's quiet good for print big size but so weak to portable.
So, the rifle became model guns that can't go outside.
Stock has 3 linear pipes that allows size change.
Also It has cheek pad. The final version of cheek pad can adjust up and down by handy turning bolt.
